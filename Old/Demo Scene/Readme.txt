We have created this simple demo for you to explore the features of the Data Dome Toolkit and test some of the settings which can affect the quality of your scene in the dome.
Within playmode or your executable build, try the following controls: 

LEFT/RIGHT - Rotate camera on Z-Axis

UP/DOWN - Rotate camera on X-Axis

[ / ] - Increase/Decrease FOV from 120-270

P - Pause camera rotation

V - Toggle Vsync (on by default)

F1 - SSAA off
F2 - 2x SSAA
F3 - 4x SSAA

Main keyboard numbers set size of cubemap:
1 - 512
2 - 1024
3 - 2048
4 - 4096
5 - 8192

R - Reset controls
